🌱 Community Garden • Latest Update

Community Garden Spring Makeover!

We are excited to tell you that our Community Garden is getting a huge makeover for the Spring. We are replacing all the raised beds with a brand new design using Railway sleepers. We are also replacing the very tired old perimeter fence around the property.

You are welcome to come and have a look and are more than welcome to help if you need something to do. Contact us on 07562 499442 or [email protected] for more information.
